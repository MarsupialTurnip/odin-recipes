Crab Mushrooms

Description

These mushrooms are stuffed full of delicious fresh, never imitated crab. They are a fluffy, and yet crispy delicious treat. You and your family will keep begging for more, which you will never receive because you didn't make enough of them; and inflation made crab far too expensive.

Ingredients

  • Crab meat,(Imitation allowed)
  • Mashed Potatoes
  • Portabella Mushrooms
  • butter

Steps

  1. Core mushrooms
  2. Cook Mashed Potatoes
  3. Cook crab in butter
  4. Mix in potatoes
  5. Stuff mushrooms with spoonfuls of crab
  6. bake at HOT and let brown
